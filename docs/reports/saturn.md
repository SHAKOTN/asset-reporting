## Judge's verdict

### ✅ What looks good

- Single-chain posture — USDat / sUSDat live on Ethereum mainnet only, no LayerZero / CCIP / Wormhole peers, zero bridge surface (cross-chain agent scored [9/10](https://etherscan.io/address/0x23238f20b894f29041f48D88eE91131C395Aaa71)).
- Doxxed founders with verifiable resumes — [Kevin Li](https://www.artemisanalytics.com/resources/kevin-li-analyst-of-the-month) (ex-Artemis / ParaFi), Sebastian Melendez (ex-Artemis stablecoin lead, [GitHub SebMelendez01](https://github.com/SebMelendez01)), Ellis Osborn — UPenn cohort.
- Tier-1 backers + institutional custody — [YZi Labs](https://protos.com/) seed, Sora Ventures angel, Anchorage Digital Bank N.A. custody implying KYB on the legal entity.
- Pendle PT hop liquidity is healthy for the size — PT-USDat <1% slippage to $3M, PT-sUSDat <1% to $1.5M via [PendleRouterStatic](https://etherscan.io/address/0x263833d47eA3fA4a30f269323aba6a107f9eB14C); both PTs expire 27-AUG-2026 collapsing to 1:1 SY redemption.
- Source-verified contracts on Etherscan — [USDat proxy](https://etherscan.io/address/0x23238f20b894f29041f48D88eE91131C395Aaa71#code), [PT-USDat](https://etherscan.io/address/0x1d69402390657308C91179aA184Bf992908c1E08#code), [PT-sUSDat](https://etherscan.io/address/0xC689F76F90fE1762fAc55983Ff25aE71033A84f7#code), no exploit / rug / sanction history.
